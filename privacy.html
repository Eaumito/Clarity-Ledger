Privacy Policy

Effective: July 10, 2026

Clarity Ledger Beta 1.2 is designed to work without accounts, analytics, advertising, or remote databases.

Data storage

Income, expense, debt, and savings entries are stored in your browser's local storage on the device you use. The app does not transmit those entries to Clarity Ledger or any server.

Clearing data

Use the “Clear my data” button in the app to remove the stored ledger data from that browser. Clearing browser site data or uninstalling the PWA may also remove it.

Hosting logs

The hosting provider may process ordinary technical request logs, such as IP address, browser type, requested page, and timestamp, for security and service operation. The app itself does not add analytics or tracking scripts.

Third parties

No Plaid connection, cloud database, advertising platform, analytics service, or external tracking script is included.

Contact

Replace feedback@eaumito.com with the project's real contact address before public launch.
