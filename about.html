About Clarity Ledger

Clarity Ledger Beta 0.2 is a privacy-first personal finance calculator built to turn scattered monthly numbers into a clear snapshot.

It calculates monthly cash flow, a break-even lifestyle number, and an estimated debt-payoff timeline using the debt avalanche method. All financial entries remain in the local browser unless the user independently copies or exports them.

The beta intentionally has no accounts, cloud sync, bank connection, analytics, advertising, or subscription system.
